MindSetting

Mindsetting was born from my desire to help others. During my years as a scientist, I witnessed students and colleagues struggle to maintain a healthy work-life balance in high-pressure environments. I was preparing to shift my career focus to mental health when I became pregnant. While this was a wonderful time in my life, the postnatal phase proved incredibly challenging, and I found myself struggling to stay in a positive mental space. My baby had trouble sleeping, and after a long journey, we discovered he had multiple food intolerances. Eventually, we found our "new normal."

However, more challenges arose. My maternity leave ended, and so did my contract with the University, leaving me a new mom without a job, grappling with an identity and confidence crisis. Like many mothers, I struggled to find flexible work, facing countless job rejections. After taking courses in yoga, meditation, nutrition, and coaching, and exploring activities I truly enjoyed, I discovered a new path. My passion for helping new moms find alignment and contentment in their lives grew stronger, leading me to create Mindsetting.

Mindsetting is centred on the continuous process of rewiring our brains and nervous systems to work in our favour. Using deep relaxation techniques, transformative conversations and targeted coaching exercises, I offer a holistic approach to balancing mind and body, guiding you toward alignment and fulfillment in your life.
