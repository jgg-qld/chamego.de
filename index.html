MindSetting

MindSetting was born from my deep desire to help others. As a scientist, I experienced firsthand the difficulty of maintaining work-life balance in uncertain and demanding conditions. My own challenges as a new mother — dysregulated nervous system, poor sleep, and a difficult return to work — led me to explore mental health and wellbeing more deeply.

To better understand and support these issues, I pursued courses in nutrition and life coaching, building on my certifications in yoga and meditation. This journey sparked a passion for helping women, especially mothers, achieve alignment and fulfillment.

Thus, MindSetting was created. We focus on rewiring your brain and nervous system to work in your favour. Through deep relaxation techniques, transformative conversations, and targeted coaching exercises, I offer a holistic approach to balance your mind and body, guiding you toward a more relaxed, aligned, and fulfilling life. My goal is to support you in gaining clarity and confidence as you take steps toward the life you envision for yourself.
