Services

Our sessions are tailored to meet your specific needs and may include:

  • Lifestyle Assessment: Explore your lifestyle, beliefs, stress levels, and confidence to understand your unique needs and challenges.
  • Mindset and Emotional Tools: Engage in exercises designed to foster acceptance, emotional regulation, and deep relaxation. These practices will help balance your nervous system and provide you with practical tools to shift your mindset and perception.
  • Yoga Instruction: Enhance flexibility and mindfulness with customized yoga practices tailored to your needs.
  • Mindfulness & Meditation: Develop awareness and inner calm through personalized guided meditations.
  • Nutrition Coaching: Discover how to nourish both body and mind with individualized nutrition guidance.

Safe and Supportive Environment: Experience a nurturing, non-judgmental space where I offer attentive, empathetic listening, guiding you in reshaping the narratives of your life.

Our sessions can be conducted in Portuguese or English, either via Zoom or in Person, based on your preference.

If you are interested, please let me know at re.mindsetting@gmail.com and I can send you more details and talk about your wants and needs in more detail.
